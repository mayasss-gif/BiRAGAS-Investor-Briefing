THE PROBLEM
— Why this is hard —

Drug discovery fails nine times in ten.

Discovering a drug is slow, hugely expensive, and usually fails — roughly 9 in 10 programs never make it. Most AI only finds patterns: it sees two things happen together, not whether one causes the other. And AI can hallucinate — state things confidently that simply aren't true.
Betting hundreds of millions on a coincidence — or on a made-up answer — is how money and years are lost.
